06 — Professional & Reference Sources
Authoritative Bodies & Data Sources
Regulatory — United Nations Agency
International Maritime Organization (IMO)
Publisher of STCW, SOLAS, MARPOL, ISM Code, and all global maritime conventions. Primary authority for the 2nd Engineer's certification and regulatory obligations.
imo.org ↗
Industry — Shipping Associations
International Chamber of Shipping (ICS)
Provides industry best practice guides including the "Tanker Safety Guide", "Ship's Bridge Procedures Guide", and ISM compliance guidance used by shipowners worldwide.
ics-shipping.org ↗
Regulatory — Seabed Governance
International Seabed Authority (ISA)
Governs all mineral resource activities in the international seabed (the Area). Engineers on survey, cable, or mining vessels must comply with ISA environmental protection regulations under UNCLOS Part XI.
isa.org.jm ↗
Ocean Data & Vessel Tracking
MarineTraffic
Real-time AIS-based vessel tracking used by shore-side technical managers and 2nd Engineers for fleet position awareness, port ETAs, and voyage performance monitoring.
marinetraffic.com ↗
Ocean Data — EU Programme
Copernicus Marine Service
European ocean monitoring programme providing sea surface temperature, current, and sea state data. Used by engineers and masters for route planning, ballast water management, and environmental reporting.
marine.copernicus.eu ↗
Technical Knowledge
Marine Insight
Leading maritime knowledge platform covering engine room procedures, equipment guides, regulatory updates, and career pathways. Widely read by engineers at all levels for practical technical reference.
marineinsight.com ↗
Industry News
The Maritime Executive
High-level industry news covering regulatory changes, shipping markets, accident investigations, and technology. Essential reading for 2nd Engineers tracking IMO regulatory updates and decarbonisation requirements.
maritime-executive.com ↗
Professional Body
The Nautical Institute
Offers the Dynamic Positioning (DP) certification, professional membership (MNI / FNI), and continuing professional development programmes. Relevant for 2nd Engineers on offshore and DP-equipped vessels.
nautinst.org ↗
Academic — Postgraduate
World Maritime University (WMU)
IMO-linked postgraduate institution in Malmö, Sweden. Offers MSc and PhD programmes in maritime affairs, port management, and ocean sustainability. Pathway for 2nd Engineers moving into management or policy roles.
wmu.se ↗
Professional Surveying
Int'l Institute of Marine Surveying (IIMS)
Provides qualifications and membership for marine surveyors. An experienced 2nd Engineer may qualify for IIMS membership and transition into independent machinery surveys, condition assessments, and casualty investigations.
iims.org.uk ↗
